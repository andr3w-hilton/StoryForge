# The Hollow Forest

**Genre:** Wilderness / Survival
**Tone:** Grim, naturalistic, isolating

A cursed overland journey through a dying forest on the empire's forgotten borderlands. Nature itself is the antagonist — the trees are wrong, the wildlife is changed, and the further you push in, the less the world outside feels real. Heavy survival flavour: foraging, shelter, tracking. Combat is dangerous and best avoided. The horror is slow and environmental rather than dungeon-crawl direct.
