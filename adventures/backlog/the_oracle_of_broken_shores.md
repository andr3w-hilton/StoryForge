# The Oracle of Broken Shores

**Genre:** Mythic / Ancient World
**Tone:** Epic, fatalistic, legend-soaked

A Bronze Age or ancient-world adventure built on myth rather than medieval fantasy. Cursed temples, oracle prophecies, sea monsters drawn from legend. You are a hero in the classical sense — not chosen, but tested. The gods are real, capricious, and unhelpful. Visually and tonally distinct from the rest of the roster: sun-bleached stone, wine-dark sea, the weight of fate.
