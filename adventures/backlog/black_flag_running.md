# Black Flag Running

**Genre:** High Seas / Piracy
**Tone:** Gritty, political, morally complex — inspired by Black Sails

Set on a Nassau-style pirate republic at the edge of collapse. The golden age of piracy is ending — the empire is tightening its grip, the crews are fracturing, and the fort that once kept the navy out is held together by deals and threats. You are a captain or first mate navigating the politics of the harbour as much as the open sea. Rival captains, merchant conspirators, colonial governors, and your own crew all have agendas. Combat at sea is brutal and decisive. The real game is who you align with and what you're willing to sacrifice to keep sailing free.

**Tone reference:** Black Sails — the Nassau political web, factions pulling in different directions, the weight of a way of life that can't last. Grounded and human, not fantastical.
