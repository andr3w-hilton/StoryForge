# One Night in Veldrath

**Genre:** Urban Intrigue / Heist
**Tone:** Tense, cunning-over-combat, morally grey

A single-night adventure set in a corrupt fantasy city. You have one job to pull off before dawn — infiltrating a noble's vault, lifting evidence, getting out clean. Faction politics, rival thieves, crooked city watch. Success depends more on who you trust and how you move than on how hard you fight. Lankhmar/Ankh-Morpork aesthetic — dense, grimy, alive.
