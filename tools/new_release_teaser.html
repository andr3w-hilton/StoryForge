The Hollow
Forest

by Andrew Hilton  ·  StoryForge
⊕

Marek went into the eastern forest three weeks ago. He was a territorial warden — methodical, careful, the kind of man who logged everything. His last message: “I think I’ve found the cause.” Then nothing. The forest on the empire’s eastern margin is dying. Not from blight, not from drought — something is wrong in a way that takes time to name. You go in to find him. The deeper you go, the clearer it becomes that the wrongness has a shape. No magic. No monsters. Survival, investigation, and a truth the forest has been keeping.
