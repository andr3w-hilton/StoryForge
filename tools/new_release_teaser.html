Black Flag
Running

by Andrew Hilton  ·  StoryForge

Nassau is a free republic. For now. The Crown's warships sit at the harbour mouth. Eleven days. No shots fired. Commodore Ashford is waiting for something specific — buried in the Shallows of Perdition, eight leagues south. A sunken galleon. A war chest. A sealed pouch called the Red Correspondence — letters that could end his career if anyone read them. You've just arrived. You have a fast sloop, a loyal crew, and a chart to the wreck. Nassau is watching to see what kind of captain you are.
