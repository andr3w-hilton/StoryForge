B1 — Gothic Horror (Valdric)

The Crypt of Count Valdric

The village of Ashford is dying. For three months its crops have withered in the fields and its people have sickened with a wasting fever that no healer can cure. The village elder, Maren — a sharp-eyed woman who remembers the old stories — knows the cause. A century ago, the necromancer Count Valdric ruled these lands from the fortress now known as Valdric Keep. When the people finally rose against him, they sealed his corpse in the crypt beneath the ruins with a signet ring of binding.

The Scavenger of New Babylon Station

New Babylon Station hangs in the dark between stars, a vast ring of corroded steel and failing systems at the ragged edge of Imperial space. Once it was a trade hub — a crossroads where a dozen species bartered fuel, ore, and information. That was before the war. You are a scavenger — a freelance wreck-rat who makes a living pulling salvage from the station's dead lower decks.

The Throne of the Hollow King

You were a knight once. That ended at Kelmarsh Bridge, where you chose mercy over orders and let the rebel lord's children flee. Your commander stripped your rank, your name, your lands. You have wandered since — hedge-knight, sellsword, nobody. Now you stand at the edge of Carragh's Foot, a village huddled in the shadow of Mount Aldric. The mountain is wrong.
